Information About the Achievement 7 Drill and Ceremonies Practical Test

Due to limitations in the regulations, the Ach. 7 is only allowed to be administered on paper for several reasons, mostly because of the fact that Problem 1 includes a writing portion, to which writing on a screen has disadvantages. Additionally, Problem 2 has a complex Change of Command portion, which would be difficult to convert to digital format.

There is also a scorecard which includes symbols to represent various positions such as the Group Commander and First Sergeant, which cannot easily be embedded in the HTML format this website is coded in.

Due to these and other reasons, Ach. 7 Drill Test has not been included in this system. Thank you for your understanding.

The alternative is to administer the test normally on paper, which can be printed or downloaded below.
