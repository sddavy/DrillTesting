Topic: Squadron Formations

Conditions: At least 3 additional cadets are needed to role play for this formation. Graded cadet will be the Squadron First Sergeant (Sq/CCF).

• Alpha Flight Sergeant (plus additional cadets to constitute the flight, if available)

• Bravo Flight Sergeant (plus additional cadets to constitute the flight, if available)

• Squadron Commander (Sq/CC)

Instruct the cadet to assume the role of first sergeant and assemble the “squadron.”

Note: The “squadron’s” ability to perform the commands is NOT relevant to this test – only the cadet’s ability to assemble the squadron is being evaluated. For flight reports, all cadets are present.
